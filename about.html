KAITLYN HAHN ABOUT CONTACT

Kaitlyn Hahn is a student at Dartmouth College, studying Studio Art and Digital Art. Growing up, her parents owned a small women's clothng line and since then, she's always been interested in visual design. She loves home (Los Angeles), light installations, Brazilian cheese bread, and music videos. She's passionate about creating and believes that it's at the core of human experiences. Kaitlyn's expecting to graduate in 2019.
